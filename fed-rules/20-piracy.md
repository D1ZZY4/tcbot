**Explanation**: To maintain a healthy community, here is a clarification regarding our piracy policy:

**Allowed**:
- ReVanced.

- Scene mods above version 5 that contain piracy content.
- Any pirated apps, mods, or piracy forms not listed in the allowed list.
- Sharing (content, SS, SR) or discussing piracy in groups, profile channels, or channels where nicknames are visible.
